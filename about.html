Our Story

We Love Nature,
And We Want to Share it.

Deniyaya is a paradise on earth, heavily forested and rich in biodiversity. For years, the stunning sceneries, the cool climate, and the welcoming people have been a well-kept secret of the south.

At Yaman Deniyaye, our mission is to introduce the unspoiled beauty of this region to the world, while preserving its natural integrity. We organize eco-friendly tours to the Sinharaja Rain Forest, Patna Waterfalls, Kanneliya and lush green tea estates.
