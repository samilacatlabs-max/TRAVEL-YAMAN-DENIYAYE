Ukgalpotha Viharaya

Ukgalpotha Viharaya is a serene Buddhist temple located in the picturesque area of Deniyaya, surrounded by lush greenery and tea plantations. This temple is renowned for its peaceful atmosphere and historical significance to the local community. Visitors are welcomed by the traditional architecture, which includes a beautiful stupa, Buddha statues, and intricate carvings that reflect the cultural heritage of the region.
